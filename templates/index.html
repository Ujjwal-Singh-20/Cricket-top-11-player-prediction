How Is the Score Calculated?


score = (
features['t20_avg'] * 0.5 +
features['t20_sr'] * 0.3 +
features['t20_wickets'] * 2 +
features['centuries'] * 1
)

t20_avg: Batting average in T20 matches
t20_sr: Batting strike rate in T20 matches
t20_wickets: Wickets taken in T20 matches
centuries: Total hundreds scored (T20, ODI, Test combined)

Weights:
The weights (0.5, 0.3, 2, 1) are custom factors, reflecting how much each stat matters for the model.
